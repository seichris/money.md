# Solana custody quick notes

Squads treasury
- Use for high-value, infrequent moves.
- Diversify owners across devices.
- Keep a clear policy that treasury does not execute routine payouts.

Ops wallet
- Keep balance low and enforce caps.
- Maintain SOL for fees and token account creation costs.

USDC considerations
- Mint is cluster-specific.
- Do not confuse devnet and mainnet-beta mints.
